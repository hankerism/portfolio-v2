# Portfolio v2.0

## Vision

Portfolio v2.0 is the evolution of my existing portfolio.

This is not a redesign.

The goal is to preserve the personality, warmth, and scrapbook/editorial aesthetic of my current portfolio while rebuilding it as a production-quality Next.js application with modern engineering practices.

The portfolio should accurately represent who I am today:

- Product Engineer
- Automation Specialist
- Operations Professional

Rather than showcasing websites alone, the portfolio should communicate my ability to design, engineer, and automate complete digital products and business systems.
